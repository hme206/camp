College Catalog

Welcome to Camp Community College

We've prepared this catalog and student handbook with your educational and career goals in mind. Here you will find the transfer and career pathways, academic programs and courses, and support services that can help you define and plan your future.

Whether your goal is to transfer to a four-year institution, gain a specialized skill to immediately enter the workforce, or to explore your entrepreneurial interests, our curriculum is designed to help you earn a degree, certificate, or industry-recognized credential. To better help you explore your career or select courses, we have categorized all of our programs into groups that have similar content areas. We encourage you to explore these areas, and prior to selecting your courses, speak with one of our highly qualified advisors, admissions staff or faculty to determine what's best for you.

We are confident that you will be pleased with your decision to enroll at Camp. Our first-rate faculty; small, cohesive classes; and hands-on instruction prepare our students to meet the many demands of today's workforce. Camp graduates excel in a variety of fields, such as health care, computer and industrial technologies, business, and many more areas of study, here in our service region and beyond.

We are committed to providing you with a rewarding learning experience at Camp, and we are extremely proud to be a part of your educational journey.
