Student & Advisor Information

The Technical Studies Associate in Applied Science Degree is designed to provide a broad foundation of general education and technological knowledge, along with a concentration in a technical field as identified by local industry needs, that will prepare the graduate to enter or advance in technical fields upon graduation.

The Technical Studies Degree will incorporate one (1) or two (2) existing certificates or career studies certificates with additional courses to create a coherent plan of study for a total of 60 – 69 credits.

Experiential credit, such as credit for prior learning, internship credit, directed study for credit, and/or apprenticeship credit may be used.

The Technical Studies Degree will be approved by a Dean, and by the Vice President of Academic and Student Development (or designee) to ensure a coherent plan of study.
