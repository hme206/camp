Steps to Admission Overview

Apply for Admission

  • Complete an application online. *There is no application fee
  • Log into to review your “To-Do List” which will help with everything from completing financial aid, getting your Student ID, and everything in between.
  • Log in with your MyCAMP user name and password to guide you to the English and math classes best suited for you. Complete the Advising Questionnaire .

Apply for Financial Aid

  • Complete and submit the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) .
  • Paul D Camp Community College's school code is 009159.

Order Transcripts and/or Score Reports (if applicable)

  • Order official transcripts from other colleges/universities attended, SAT, ACT, or GED scores and send to transcripts@pdc.edu or Camp Community College, ATTN: College Registrar, 100 N College Dr., Franklin, VA 23851.
  • Any transferrable credit will be evaluated and posted to your Camp academic record upon enrollment.

If you attended another college in the Virginia Community College System, you do not need to send an official transcript to CAMP. Please fill out the VCCS Evaluation form and submit to transcripts@pdc.edu.

Meet with an Enrollment Navigator/Advisor

  • An enrollment navigator/advisor will discuss your entrance requirements and set up your first semester courses using direct enrollment.
  • Register for classes in person or online at MyCamp.
  • For the advising hours to meet with an enrollment navigator/advisor please visit: Advising for New Students.

Participate in the online New Student Orientation, included in your To-Do List

  • Follow the link and log into Navigate .

Pay for Classes (Camp is very affordable!)

  • Pay tuition and fees by the deadline.
  • Pay in person at the Cashier's Office.
  • Pay by credit card (Visa or MasterCard) online through MyCamp.

Obtain Your Picture ID

  • Obtain your picture student ID from the Library.
  • *Memorize your student ID number.
